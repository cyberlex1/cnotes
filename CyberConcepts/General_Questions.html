Work with team

During testing, I provide regular updates on progress or critical findings. After the test, I deliver a detailed report outlining vulnerabilities, risk levels (e.g., CVSS), exploitation steps, and remediation guidance. I also participate in debrief meetings to explain technical findings in a clear and actionable way, tailored to both technical and non-technical audiences.
